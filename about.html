Kristyn Kubiak is a professional artist based in Massachusetts. Her mastery of color and the human figure help her express complex feelings and emotions.

Recent projects include The Prismatic Macabre - a solo art exhibition featuring acrylic paintings embellished with gold leaf exploring grief, joy, and depression. She is currently studying animation and working on a new exhibition to be released in the fall of 2023.
